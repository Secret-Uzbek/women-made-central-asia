# PUBLICATION RULE

Publication boundary for `women-made-central-asia`.

## Public surface

This repository is public-facing and should prioritize:

- readable research narratives;
- clearly scoped essays;
- sourced historical interpretation;
- respectful public communication.

## Keep out of the public layer

Do not place the following here unless heavily curated and necessary:

- raw ingestion files;
- unstable archive exports;
- noisy AI drafts;
- unsourced claims;
- material that can expose living people or communities to avoidable harm.

## Routing rule

If material is primarily evidence or structured source mapping, it belongs in
the corpus repository instead of this public narrative layer.

# CODE OF CONDUCT

This repository studies women as civilizational operators in Central Asia.
Its subject requires seriousness, respect, and protection from exploitation.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful in language and interpretation;
- take women, families, ritual knowledge, and oral continuity seriously;
- welcome careful disagreement;
- protect living communities from demeaning or extractive behavior.

## Unacceptable behavior

- misogyny, harassment, ridicule, or sexualized framing;
- racist or colonial dismissal of Central Asian knowledge systems;
- exploitative extraction of cultural material without context;
- fabricated evidence;
- abusive use of AI to mass-produce false narratives.

## Priority protection

Anything that could harm:

- children,
- women,
- living communities,
- sacred or culturally sensitive material,

may be removed or restricted immediately.

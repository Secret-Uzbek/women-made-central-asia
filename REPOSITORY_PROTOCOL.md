# REPOSITORY PROTOCOL

Operational protocol for `women-made-central-asia`.

## Before change

- verify source grounding;
- check for cultural sensitivity;
- check for risk of flattening named and unnamed women into decorative tropes.

## During change

- keep the distinction between evidence and interpretation visible;
- preserve readability for public audiences;
- avoid sensationalism and invented certainty.

## After change

- ensure the contribution still belongs in the public narrative layer;
- move evidence-heavy additions to the corpus repository when appropriate;
- keep governance, safety, and convention links intact.

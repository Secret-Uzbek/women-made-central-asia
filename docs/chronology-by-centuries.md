# Chronology by Centuries

## Deep antiquity
- early settled and proto-urban cultures in Central Asia;
- ritual, domestic, and material worlds before formal state chronicles;
- future focus: women in protohistory, ornament, burial, household continuity.

## 1st millennium BCE
- Saka and Massagetae worlds;
- Tomyris as a key figure of female rulership and political memory;
- Bactria, Sogdiana, Khorezm as major civilizational zones.

## Turkic khaganate era
- khatuns and clan politics;
- legitimacy, kinship, succession, alliance;
- women in steppe political order.

## Timurid era
- Saray Mulk Khanum and Bibi Khanum memory layer;
- women in architecture, court, dynastic memory, sacred geography.

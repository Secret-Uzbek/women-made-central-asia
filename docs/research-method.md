# Research Method

## Principle 1 — layered evidence

This project works with multiple evidence layers:
- historical scholarship,
- chronology,
- architectural memory,
- material culture,
- oral tradition,
- literary imagination,
- ontological framing through FMP and Invisible Shadow.

## Principle 2 — different sources, different status

Not all sources are equal.

### Primary scholarly-historical layer
- major historical books,
- chronology works,
- archaeology,
- material culture studies,
- dynastic-legitimacy studies.

### Secondary interpretive layer
- guidebooks,
- city books,
- popular syntheses,
- architectural atlases.

### Oral and legendary layer
- legends,
- local memory,
- non-archival tradition.

### Conceptual layer
- FMP,
- NULLO,
- PLT,
- UCOMM,
- EUO,
- Invisible Shadow.

# Women and Material Civilization

The history of women in Central Asia cannot be reduced to dynastic biography.
A huge part of women’s civilizational role lies in the material world.

## Core areas
- textiles,
- adornment,
- food,
- markets,
- architecture.

## Strong claim

Material civilization is not an accessory to real history.
It is one of the main places where women’s historical centrality becomes visible.
